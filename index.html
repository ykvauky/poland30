Wawel Castle
features a royal palace with grand rooms,
a cathedral housing kings’ tombs,
historic caves, the legendary dragon statue,
and exhibitions of ancient jewels and weapons. It’s a central and impressive historic site in Kraków.

Przełom Dunajca
A scenic river gorge offering traditional wooden raft rides between towering cliffs and lush forests.
It’s a popular nature escape especially during spring and summer.

Ice Park Kraków
An open-air ice skating park in Kraków operating only during winter.
It features colorful lights, music, and a cozy atmosphere, with skate rentals available.
Perfect for beginners and families.
